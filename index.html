About Me

I am an Electronics and Communication Engineering graduate from Nepal Engineering College (affiliated with Pokhara University), and currently serving as a Summer Research Fellow in AI/ML at the Nepalese Society of Student Researchers (NSSR). My research lies at the intersection of computer vision, mixed/augmented reality, and AI-driven assistive technologies. I focus on designing inclusive learning tools that support children with Autism Spectrum Disorder, combining real-time visual perception, adaptive interaction design, and user-centered evaluation to create scalable, evidence-informed interventions.

As a quick learner with a strong belief in hard work, I am always eager to explore new domains, expand my skills, and apply innovative solutions to real-world problems.
